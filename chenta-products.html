Helical Gear Reducer
(R-Series)

Chenta Helical Gear Reducer is designed according to the "German Institute for Standardization" (DIN) regulation allowing interchangeability with international leading brands, while keeping structure rigidity and compactness. The helical gears provide high efficiency with high stability and low noise level, making it suitable for wide range of applications

Features
  • 1 - 3 stages gear design
  • Available in single dan double reduction
  • Ratio 1/1.3 - 1/27788
  • Power ranges from 1/4HP to 150HP
  • Cast iron housing strengthen the protection against crash
  • Double bearing supported input pinion provides stability at high speed
  • Pinion and gears are made with 20CrMo alloy steel plus carburizing heat treatment to enhance performance
  • Vertical and horizontal mounting with various input mechanism
  • Input flange is compatible with IEC or NEMA motor
  • Various choices of mounting position

Helical Bevel Gear Reducer (K-Series)

Chenta Helical Bevel Gear Reducer is designed according to the "German Institute for Standardization" (DIN) regulation allowing interchangeability with international leading brands, while keeping structure rigidity and compactness. The combination of highly efficient helical gears and smooth transmission of worm gears ensures higher stability and lower noise compared to regular helical gear units.

Features
  • 90 degree angle transmission
  • Available in single dan double reduction
  • Ratio 1/5 - 1:215, providing more accommodation than worm gear speed reducer
  • Power ranges from 1/4HP to 60HP
  • Pinion, gears and worm shafts are made with 20CrMo alloy steel plus carbonized heat treatment
  • Aluminium bronze worm wheel offers high strength and endurance
  • Vertical and horizontal mounting with various input mechanism
  • Input flange is compatible with IEC or NEMA motor
  • Various choices of mounting position

Chenta Helical Bevel Gear Reducer has been utilized in many applications, including water gate automation and flood control projects.

Worm Gear Reducer (W/G-Series)

Chenta Worm Gear is made of high-quality material allowing for excellent durability and long lasting service life.

Features
  • 90 degree angle transmission 
  • Available in single dan double reduction
  • Ratio 1/5 - 1/3600
  • Power ranges from 1/4HP to 100HP

    • below size 300 — Medium Carbon Steel (S45C) with harden heat treatment and grinding on threads

  • Aluminium Bronze (ALBC3) worm wheel for excellent durability
  • Robust housing:
    •     below size 300 — Grey Iron (FC20)
    • size 300 and above — Ductile Cast Iron (FCD45)
