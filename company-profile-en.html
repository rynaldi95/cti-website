Company Profile

Established in 2007, Chenta Transmission Indonesia is a trusted distributor and subsidiary of a Taiwanese based company, Chenta Precision Machinery Industrial Inc. We are proud to represent the renowned quality of Chenta's transmission solutions to the Indonesian market. The product ranges from in-line helical gear reducer, helical bevel gear reducer, worm gear, parallel shaft gear reducer and other application tailored gearboxes.

On top of that, we are also partnering with Transcyko Transmission Machinery Co., Ltd. to provide the industry with an even wider range of drive units, such as cycloidal speed reducer, large TSG gearbox, variable speed drive and planetary gear reducer.

We are devoted to providing excellent customer service and satisfying our customers’ needs. Our team is well-trained to provide the best solutions for our customers’ gearbox needs with a competitive price. With a wide range of gearboxes and an extensive network, you can rest assured that your gearbox needs are taken care of.
